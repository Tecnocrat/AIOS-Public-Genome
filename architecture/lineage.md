# Lineage

**AIOS Architectural Layer — Evolutionary Record**

---

## Overview

Lineage is the **historical record** of the organism's evolution.  
Every structural change, crystallization, and architectural decision is traced here.

Lineage serves three purposes:

1. **Accountability** — who proposed what, and when  
2. **Traceability** — how the organism arrived at its current state  
3. **Coherence** — ensuring mutations don't contradict prior decisions  

---

## Lineage Record Format

Each lineage entry should contain:

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Epoch** | Architectural version at time of entry |
| **Date** | Timestamp of crystallization |
| **Agent** | Originating agent or operator |
| **Type** | Mutation, selection, crystallization, or propagation |
| **Description** | What changed and why |
| **References** | Related genome sections, crystals, or proposals |
